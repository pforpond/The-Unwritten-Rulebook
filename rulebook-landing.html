What is The Unwritten Rule Book?

Every Dead by Daylight player knows there are unspoken rules in the fog. Don't camp, don't tunnel, give hatch to the last survivor... or do you? This site will soon be the definitive collection of all those community-created "rules" that have evolved over the years of gameplay.

Stay tuned as we prepare to launch a platform where you can browse, submit, and vote on the unwritten rules of Dead by Daylight.

The Unwritten Rule Book is a fan-made project and is not affiliated with Behaviour Interactive or Dead by Daylight.
